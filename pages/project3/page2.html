About this Dataset

The view shows volunteer organizations in different areas of New York, with different colors representing different areas, such as Manhattan, Brooklyn, Flushing, Long Island City, etc. You can click on each grid and learn the mission statement of each organization, and click again on the button in the lower right corner to have the details pop up. You can select the organization you are interested in and learn about its volunteer opportunities.
